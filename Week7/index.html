Beyond The Screen



Welcome to Beyond The Screen! This is a safe space where we watch movies and have thorough discussions on how the film impacted us. Kind of like a book club, but for films!

How Does This Work?

I'll watch a movie every week or so based off of your recommendations. After viewing the film I'll give my personal analysis and thoughts on it. I provide a community response section as well so you can chime in on the conversation!

Let's watch some films together!
